## Reflection

### 1. What went well during this unit?

Planning went smoothly as a team. We aligned quickly on the app concept — a developer Q&A forum — and were able to define the core features, data model, and user flows without much back-and-forth. The ERD came together naturally once we mapped out the user stories.

### 2. What were some challenges your group faced in this unit?

Coordinating schedules to work synchronously was difficult. We also spent extra time deciding how to scope the MVP versus stretch features, since there were many AI-powered ideas we wanted to include but needed to deprioritize to keep the project manageable.

### 3. What additional support will you need in upcoming units as you continue to work on your final project?

We would benefit from guidance on setting up the PostgreSQL database with the relational schema we designed, and on how to structure the Express API routes to support filtering and sorting on the feed. Any examples of connecting a React frontend to a REST backend with authentication would also be helpful.
